Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 February 2026

Music Life ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application for iOS and Android that helps musicians tune instruments, practise, and analyse chords. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the app.

1. Audio Data

Music Life uses your device's microphone solely for real-time pitch detection and chord analysis. Audio is processed entirely on-device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server or third party. The app does not retain any audio samples after each analysis frame is processed.

Microphone access is requested only when you open the Tuner or Chord Analyser screen and is released as soon as you leave that screen.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share:

  • Audio recordings or microphone samples
  • Personal identification information (name, email, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to third-party services

3. Local Storage

The app stores your in-app preferences (e.g., dark mode, reference pitch calibration, practice log entries) in local device storage using the platform's standard secure storage APIs. This data never leaves your device and is removed when you uninstall the app.

4. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Microphone – required for pitch detection and chord analysis. Access is used only while the relevant screen is active.

You can revoke microphone permission at any time in your device settings. Revoking it disables pitch detection and chord analysis features.

5. Third-Party Services

Music Life does not integrate any third-party SDKs that collect personal data (e.g., no advertising networks, no analytics services, no social- login libraries).

6. Children's Privacy

Music Life does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Because the app collects no personal data at all, it is safe to use by all age groups.

7. GDPR & Rights of Users in the EEA

Because we do not collect personal data, there is no data to access, rectify, erase, or port. If you have questions about your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you may contact us at the address below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
